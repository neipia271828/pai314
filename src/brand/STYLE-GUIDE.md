# Pai314 Brand Style Guide

> Version 1.0.0 | Last Updated: 2026-02-21

---

## Brand Overview

**Pai314** is a personal brand rooted in the elegance of mathematics and the infinite nature of pi ($\pi$). The visual identity draws from the beauty of irrational numbers, concentric spirals, and geometric precision. The brand conveys intellectual depth, creative exploration, and a love for the patterns that underlie our universe.

### Brand Philosophy

- **Infinite Curiosity** --- Like the digits of pi, exploration never ends
- **Precision & Beauty** --- Mathematics is both exact and aesthetic
- **Depth Over Surface** --- Dark, rich palettes reflect thoughtfulness and substance

### Brand Keywords

`mathematical` `geometric` `precise` `infinite` `elegant` `deep` `exploratory`

| Icon | `円周率.png` | Favicons, profile pictures, small formats |

### Clear Space

Maintain a minimum clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "π" symbol in the logo. No other elements should intrude into this zone.

### Minimum Size

- **Digital**: 32px height minimum (icon variant)
- **Print**: 10mm height minimum

### Logo Don'ts

- Do not rotate the logo
- Do not stretch or distort proportions
- Do not change logo colors outside approved palette
- Do not place on busy/cluttered backgrounds without sufficient contrast
- Do not add drop shadows or effects to the logo

## Usage Guidelines

### Do's

- Use the dark theme as the default presentation
- Maintain generous whitespace around the logo and key elements
- Use Playfair Display for headings to echo the elegance of the pi symbol
- Use monospace fonts for displaying numbers, code, and mathematical content
- Apply the teal glow effect sparingly for emphasis
- Pair Primary Teal with Secondary Navy for maximum brand recognition
- Use geometric patterns (circles, spirals) as decorative elements

### Don'ts

- Don't use the logo below minimum size guidelines
- Don't place the logo on low-contrast backgrounds
- Don't use more than 3 colors from the palette simultaneously in small UI elements
- Don't substitute brand fonts with similar alternatives without approval
- Don't use bright, saturated backgrounds --- the brand is dark-first
- Don't use the teal glow effect on every element; reserve it for focal points
- Don't mix serif and sans-serif in the same line of text

### Tone of Voice

- **Curious**: Ask questions, explore ideas
- **Precise**: Be accurate, respect the details
- **Warm**: Despite the mathematical theme, remain approachable
- **Thoughtful**: Take time to explain, don't rush
